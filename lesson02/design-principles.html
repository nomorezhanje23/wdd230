When designing, the relationship between items on a page and site is critical for conveying a message and purpose to the website visitors. Proximity deals with the placement of items and content on a page to establish the relative strength of a relationship between items.

Contrast

Uniliver

uniliver.com

Contrast is designing a distinct difference between foreground and background colors in order to enhance readability. Contrast does not only deal with colors but also blends into visual hierarchy of font sizes, image placement, and content.
