Target Audience

The site is targeted towards people coming to visit, and stay in Cape Town. There are lots of touristic places to visit in the Cape Town, so information that can be found on this site is key when visting and even staying for a little while in Cape Town. Anyone from a high school kid age to grandparents can find all enjoyment and the diverse entertainment provided at thier convient expense in and around the Mother City. The site also helps visitors keep updated with visitor's information, weather reports, and activies or festivals in Cape Town.

Persona 1: Taps

Taps is an online BYUI student who enjoys taking walks at the beach and driving around the coast of Cape Town. He is currently working as a peer mentor at Bloom to help pay for his education and day to day activities. He enjoys being outdoors during summer in Cape Town, and especially watching the sunset at the beach. When it comes to checking the weather he uses his phone a lot and plans ahead on his calendar to balance everything he does.

Persona 2: Hawa

Hawa enjoys visiting Cape Town for many reasons. To get away from work, enjoy the scenary, paticipate in the markerts, hike, food and the people. She lives just outside of Cape Town and so does most of her family members, her best place to meet with everyone is in Cape Town where they can all do a lot of things together. Being able to plan for such special momements is important to her and knowing where is a good spot or time to meet while keeping up with the weather is very crucial to her as family is priority in her life.
